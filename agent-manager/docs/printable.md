# War Cleric Level 3 (D&D 2014) – Combat Reference

## Character Snapshot

STR 16 (+3) | WIS 18 (+4)  
AC 18 (Chain Mail + Shield)  
Weapon: +1 Warhammer (+6 to hit, 1d8+4)  
Spell Save DC: 14 | Spell Attack: +6  
War Priest: 4/short rest  
Channel Divinity: 1/rest

---

# 1st-Level Spells (Best Picks)

## Bless ⭐ (Core Buff)

Effect: +1d4 to attacks and saves (3 targets)  
Concentration, 1 minute

Use:

- Start of important fights
- Boost party damage and survival

---

## Healing Word ⭐ (Emergency Heal)

Effect: 1d4 +4 healing  
Bonus Action, 60 ft

Use:

- Revive allies at 0 HP
- Heal without losing your attack

---

## Guiding Bolt ⭐ (Burst Damage)

Damage: 4d6 radiant  
Next attack vs target has advantage

Use:

- Opening attack
- Helping allies land big hits

---

## Shield of Faith

Effect: +2 AC (AC 18 → 20)  
Concentration

Use:

- Defensive fights
- Tanking situations

---

## Command

Effect: Force 1-word action (WIS save)

Use:

- Control enemies
- Disrupt dangerous targets

---

# 2nd-Level Spells (Best Picks)

## Spiritual Weapon ⭐ (Core Damage Engine)

Damage: 1d8 +4 (bonus action)  
Duration: 10 rounds  
No concentration

Use:

- Every serious fight
- Adds damage every round

---

## Aid ⭐ (Pre-Buff)

Effect: +5 max HP (3 targets)  
Duration: 8 hours

Use:

- Before combat
- Increases party survivability

---

## Hold Person ⭐ (Control)

Effect: Paralyzed (WIS save)

Paralyzed =

- Cannot move
- Melee hits = auto crit

Use:

- Humanoid bosses
- High-value targets

---

## Lesser Restoration

Removes:

- Poisoned, blinded, deafened, paralyzed

Use:

- Situational support

---

# Combat Rotation

## 🥇 Standard Fight (3+ rounds)

### Round 1

Action: Bless  
Bonus: War Priest attack

---

### Round 2

Action: Warhammer attack  
Bonus: Spiritual Weapon (attack)

---

### Round 3+

Action: Warhammer attack  
Bonus: Spiritual Weapon attack

---

## Damage Per Round

Warhammer: 1d8 +4  
Spiritual Weapon: 1d8 +4

Total:
2d8 +8 (~17 avg)

---

## ⚡ Short Fight (1–2 rounds)

### Round 1–2

Action: Warhammer attack  
Bonus: War Priest attack

Damage:
2d8 +8 per round

---

## 🎯 Ranged Option

Action: Guiding Bolt (4d6)  
Bonus: War Priest attack (if in range)

---

## ❤️ Emergency Healing

Bonus: Healing Word  
Action: Warhammer attack

---

# Key Abilities

## Guided Strike (Channel Divinity)

Add +10 to hit after rolling

Use:

- Boss fights
- Missed critical attacks

---

## War Priest

Bonus attack uses: 4

Use:

- Early rounds
- Burst damage

---

# Quick Combat Rules

✔ Long fight → Bless + Spiritual Weapon  
✔ Short fight → Double attack  
✔ Ally down → Healing Word  
✔ Boss → Guided Strike
